About Us

NANAK is a national organization based in 8 states of India which has been set up with a vision to harness the full potential of individuals and institutions through knowledge creation and dissemination with the goal of empowering the disadvantaged.

Learn more

Our Projects

NANAK is actively working in the field of education, women empowerment, environmental awareness and livelihood generation. The organization believes in taking a holistic approach to development through empowering all facets of society.

Our Flagship Project

Dr. RadhikaRaman Public School was inaugurated in Majhui Village of Bihar in 2012 after it was clear that the deplorable state of childhood education in the village needed conserted efforts so that the future of the region's children could be improved.
